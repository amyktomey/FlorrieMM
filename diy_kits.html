DIY Potion Kit

Make Your Own Mystical Potion

Have you been wanting to make your own potion, but don't know where to start? Check out Florrie's new DIY Potion Kit available in two sizes, small and large.

Choose what comes in your kit!

  • Bottle: Select from a variety of bottles, small or large.
  • Potion Color: select the main color for your potion
  • Mica powder: select one or more colors for swirl and change
  • Wax: what color do you want on top of your bottle?
  • Stamp: decorative stamp for the wax
  • Cord: the wrap around the neck of the bottle
  • Bottle decor: Bead and frame, charm:
  • Label: several to choose from
All kits include: one bottle of Florrie's potion water, mini scoop for mica powder, glue gun for wax, handle for stamp, E600 to seal bottle lid... all in a handy storage box. Please do not store the glue gun when it is hot.
