DIY Potion Kit

Design Your Own Mystical Potion

Have you been wanting to design your own potion, but don't know where to start? Check out Florrie's new DIY Potion Kits - available in two sizes, small and large.

Choose what comes in your kit!

  • Bottle: Select from a variety of bottles, small or large.
  • Potion Color: select the main color for your potion
  • Mica powder: select up to 3 colors for swirl and change
  • Wax: what color do you want on top of your bottle?
  • Seal: decorative stamp for the wax
  • Cord: the wrap around the neck of the bottle
  • Bottle decor: Bead and frame, charm:
  • Label: designed to fit the bottle of your choice
All kits include: one bottle of Florrie's special potion water, charm and bead for decoration, mini scoop for mica powder, glue gun for wax, handle for stamp, E600 to seal bottle lid... all in a handy storage box. **Please do not store the glue gun when it is hot.**
Everything you need to create your one-of-a-kind potion at home EXCEPT scissors and paper towel.
Please watch the tutorial completely before assembling your potion. If you need to rinse out your bottle, use rubbing alcohol. Do not add tap water to Florrie's special formula. Her blend comes with mold deterrent, and is specially formulated to make the best potion.
Remember, these potions are NOT for drinking.
