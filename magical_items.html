Homebrew Feat Charms are coming soon!

~~~Inspired by the world of Dungeons and Dragons, these homebrew feat charms draw power from magical sources, based on the hair used to create them. Made into keychains to prevent loss, they give the holder a +2 to skill checks and a magical effect. Regardless of your class, these charms will add an egde to your encounters.

~~~~ What will give you the best benefit?

  • Banshee ~ +2 on Intimidation and advantage against Deception
  • Leprechan ~ +2 on Sleight of Hand, once per long rest - reroll on dex saving rolls under ten.
  • Ogre ~ +2 on Athletics and advantacge on Strength saving throw
  • Qilin ~ +2 to AC, if dowmed HP drops to 1, not 0.
  • Unicorn ~ +2 to Persuasion, advantage against being charmed.
