Florrie's Mystical Potions

Liquid fantasy for magical moments

All magic comes with a price. Florrie's potions have been designed with that thought in mind. The misuse of magic can have dire consequences.

Since every potion is prepared by hand... colors/shimmers may vary from batch to batch. Bottle styles may vary based on availability.


Choose with care...

  • Lover's Bane ~ Passion devoid of love breeds dangerous obsession
  • Midnight Tempest ~ Excessive use will cause prenatural phenomena
  • Mermaid Breath ~ Prolonged use results in delusions and drowning.
  • Lunaria Elixer ~ Excision before expiration results in death.
  • Death's Due ~ Warning: The reaper will have his due.
  • Forrie's Fairy Wine ~ Delightful for fairies and magical kin. Humankind could lose their memories after a thimble full.
